# Wonderland Training Tracker

Wonderland Training Tracker is a personal web application designed to help prepare for hiking the Wonderland Trail around Mount Rainier.

It focuses on structured, progressive training using incline treadmill workouts, loaded pack sessions, and strength work. The app tracks training volume, effort, and fatigue to support long-distance, multi-day mountain hiking preparation.

---

## Purpose

The goal of this app is to help users:

- Build endurance for sustained climbing and descending
- Progressively increase pack weight safely
- Monitor weekly training volume and consistency
- Reduce injury risk through workload tracking
- Arrive at the trail physically prepared

It is optimized for people who train at sea level and rely on treadmills and indoor facilities to simulate elevation.

---

## Key Features

- Workout logging for incline, flat, strength, and recovery sessions
- Automatic calculation of simulated vertical gain
- Weekly training targets based on training phase
- Readiness score to estimate trail preparedness
- Training stress tracking using duration, effort, and load
- Overuse and undertraining warning flags
- 12-week trend visualization
- Persistent local training history

---

## Training Approach

The app organizes training into four phases:

- **Base** – Build aerobic capacity and basic strength
- **Build** – Increase climbing volume and pack weight
- **Peak** – Simulate multi-day trail demands
- **Taper** – Reduce volume and recover before the hike

Each phase includes recommended weekly targets for vertical gain, session duration, and pack weight.

---

## Who This Is For

This app is intended for:

- Hikers preparing for the Wonderland Trail
- Long-distance backpackers
- Sea-level athletes training for mountain terrain
- Users who want structured, data-driven preparation

It is built for individual use rather than social fitness tracking.

---

## Philosophy

This app emphasizes:

- Consistency over intensity
- Progressive overload
- Honest effort tracking
- Injury prevention
- Long-term durability

It prioritizes preparation quality over motivation features.

---

## Disclaimer

This application is a personal training aid. It does not replace professional medical, fitness, or outdoor safety advice. Users are responsible for managing their own health, safety, and trail conditions.
